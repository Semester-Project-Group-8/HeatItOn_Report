\subsection*{Problem Statement}

\noindent HeatItOn is a utility in the city of Heating that supplies heat to approximately 1600 buildings. HeatItOn currently plans heat production manually, which makes it hard to always choose the cheapest combination of production units. The problem is therefore to develop an optimization system that can automatically create heat production schedules that always meet the heat demand while minimizing total costs and maximizing profit from electricity production/consumption.
